AS/RS

Survey of the possible options for the automation of a deposit, through the use of sensors and automation technology. The system will take charge of receiving / dispatching the pallets that arrive or should be removed from the warehouse automatically as described below: Warehouse has N aisles, and N robots (one robot in each aisle) responsible for the loading and unloading of pallets in the corridor that corresponds to them. In addition the warehouse has a single site dedicated to the reception / dispatch of pallets to which the robots will have to be able to move. When a new pallet arrives on the conveyor belt, the system receives three values that will be useful to locate the new pallet.
